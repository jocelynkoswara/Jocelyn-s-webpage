Pride and Prejudice

Pride and Prejudice written by Jane Austen, is amongst one of the best romance novels. The written tale of love, society, status and adversity amongst the characters is what makes Pride and Prejudice such a timless tale. Within the book, readers are able to observe characters as if they were one themselves as Austen weaves her tapestry with the subtle nuances of love and affection
